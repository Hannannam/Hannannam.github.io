◆ MISSION BRIEFING - THE PROBLEM
Amharic is nearly invisible on mainstream learning platforms like Duolingo.
Amharic is virtually invisible on mainstream platforms like Duolingo. Existing resources are fragmented everywhere on the internet. One site might list grammar rules while another shows isolated vocabulary, with no cohesive thread. This lack of a centralized, lesson-based curriculum forces learners to waste mental energy organizing information rather than mastering it. Without a structured roadmap from zero to fluency, the path to connecting with Amharic culture becomes a high-friction slog that discourages even the most motivated students.
⚠ THE DEBUFF
DEBUFF · 01

DEBUFF · 02

DEBUFF · 03

◆ STRATEGY - THE SOLUTION
CHOSEN APPROACH
I built Amharic Joy - a centralized hub blending the science of learning with modern AI technology.
I built Amharic Joy as a comprehensive ecosystem that bridges the gap between learning science and modern technology. Using Backward Design, I started with the end goal: fluent communication and deep cultural immersion. I leveraged AI for rapid prototyping and UI iteration, allowing me to focus on the instructional architecture. By applying the Science of Learning (such as spaced repetition and multi-modal reinforcement), I crafted a user experience designed for maximum retention. The platform serves as a centralized hub, housing everything from diagnostic roadmaps to immersive practice tools in one cohesive journey.

◇ PLATFORM FEATURES
PLATFORM MECHANICS
◆ FEATURE 01 · DASHBOARD & MOTIVATION

A Trusted Hub for Language Growth

I designed the dashboard to establish immediate trust and credibility, mimicking the stability of a professional learning environment. Beyond aesthetics, the interface is built to drive consistent habit formation. I implemented dynamic lesson streaks and a "Daily Word" spotlight to lower the barrier to entry, providing learners with predictable, low-stakes wins that build long-term momentum and psychological safety.
◆ FEATURE 02 · 20-UNIT COURSE PATH

A Guided Journey to Mastery

I synthesized the Peace Corps Amharic Training Manual into a digital 20-unit roadmap. To manage cognitive load, I designed a visual status system inspired by RPG quest logs: Black for unvisited, Yellow for in-progress, and Green for mastery. This enables learners to see their progression at a glance and identifies units ready for review, ensuring that foundational concepts are reinforced before moving to complex grammar.
◆ FEATURE 03 · THE ULTIMATE PRACTICE HUB

Multi-Modal Learning Engine

The Practice Hub is the engine of the platform, hosting specialized tools to target different levels of cognitive processing. Every artifact is an intentional learning asset designed to build the neurological connections required for native recall. This multi-modal approach ensures that learners aren't just memorizing words, but are developing the skills required for listening, writing, and active communication.
ARTIFACT: AMHARIC TYPER
A rapid-fire game for building phonetic recall and typing speed.
ARTIFACT: FIDEL WRITER
Precision practice for the Ge'ez script and stroke order.
ARTIFACT: LISTENING LAB
Native speaker immersion via songs, podcasts, and lesson audio.
ARTIFACT: VIRTUAL KEYBOARD
Zero-install Amharic input method for seamless participation.
◆ FEATURE 04 · READING & SCENARIO PRACTICE

Immersive Contextual Application

Reading practice is moved from static text to scenario-based stories with synchronized audio support for native-speaker immersion. I implemented a one-click "Plus" button as a bridge to active study; this allows learners to capture emerging vocabulary in real-time and instantly export them to personalized flashcard decks, closing the gap between reading comprehension and vocabulary acquisition.
◆ FEATURE 05 · SCHEDULER & FEEDBACK

Sustainable Mastery & Feedback Loops

To prevent learner burnout, I built tools for managing cognitive load, such as the Weekly Scheduler. This allows students to pace their own journey based on personal availability. Furthermore, the entire platform is an evolving system; I've integrated feedback loops that capture learner pain points, allowing for data-driven updates that ensure the content remains relevant, accurate, and impactful.
SYSTEM: WEEKLY SCHEDULER
