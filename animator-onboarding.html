▸ MISSION BRIEFING - THE PROBLEM
Sino Studio chose to follow the Japanese animation pipeline - but most of its in-house animators had only ever encountered it through word of mouth.
Vietnam has no formal animation school operating at industry level. The studio's process became an unstable hybrid - Western terminology stitched together with fragments of Japanese workflow, learned informally and inconsistently across the team. The result was a cascade of recurring problems: improper techniques, incorrect line weight, inaccurate colors, and constant rework.
⚠ ACTIVE DEBUFFS ON PARTY
DEBUFF · 01
No formal training infrastructure or dedicated trainer.
DEBUFF · 02
Pipeline terminology fragmented across three languages.
DEBUFF · 03
Zero budget allocated for onboarding materials.
◆ STRATEGY - THE SOLUTION
CHOSEN APPROACH
A self-paced training that looks and feels like the work itself.
I designed an interactive visual novel in Ren'Py - branching by role, free to deploy, simulating the actual Clip Studio Paint environment so practicing the workflow is indistinguishable from doing the job.
✦ BUFFS GAINED
3 branching learning paths
Zero-cost engine (Ren'Py)
In-engine CSP simulation
Cert-gated 80% pass rule
