◇ BACKSTORY
I believe the strength of a society is shaped by its education system, yet many people overlook just how powerful learning can be. I want to change that.

My goal led me to study Psychology, where I explored what makes people tick and what makes learning truly “click.” That foundation carried me into a Master’s program in Instructional Design, where I discovered my passion for blending technology, aesthetics, and pedagogy to create engaging, intuitive learning experiences.
I’m a naturally curious person, fascinated by everything, especially technology and how we can use it to empower others.

I am especially interested in how aesthetics and visuals affect the way we learn. My strength lies in making lessons fun and visually appealing through sound and illustration.
Ultimately, my goal is to make a meaningful impact on education in Vietnam.

One day, I hope to design an effective K–12 curriculum and build an innovative school that helps students reach their full potential. I’m here to shape the future of learning, one lesson at a time.
