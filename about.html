▸ BACKSTORY
The state of society is decided by education. But ironically, as a society, we like to sleep on education. Aren’t you tempted to change that fact?

I want to create a world where people love to learn!
So in my undergrad, I majored in psychology to understand what makes people curious, what makes something “click”. Then, I attended a master’s program in Instructional Design and Learning Technologies to learn how to turn that understanding into exciting and engaging learning experiences!

My biggest strength is making lessons fun and visually appealing through sound, illustration, and thoughtful design.

I’m always excited to learn new things and explore better ways to make a positive impact on education!
